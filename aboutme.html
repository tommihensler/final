About Me

I am an emerging Media Professional. I am currently studying Applied Media Communications at the Univeristy of Cincinnati.

I grew up in Cincinati Ohio.

I attended Taylor High School in Cleves, Ohio.

I lived in Columbus, Ohio for several years where I worked primarily in the Beauty Industry. I worked for brands such as Sephora (LVMH) as well as Jo Malone London, an Estee Lauder brand.

While at Jo Malone London, I rediscoverd my love for Content Creation, and Marketing.

When I moved back to Cincinnati to be closer to family, I continued this passion with my position as a Leasing Consultant in multi-family housing for PLK Communities.

While at PLK, I was also primarily responsible for our social media marketing at my site, Factory 52 Apartments. While I was there I was able to greatly grow the accounts social media presence. @factory52apartments

That leads to me today, back in school, strengthening my skills in production!
